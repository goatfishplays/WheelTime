\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Min-\/heap of contexts waiting until a steady-\/clock wake time. 

Used by the \doxylink{class_application_1_1_scheduler}{Scheduler} so workers never sleep for delays. The scheduler parks Delayed (or not-\/yet-\/started) contexts here and sleeps on its mailbox until the earliest wake, a new event, or stop.

Not thread-\/safe — owned and used only by the scheduler thread.
